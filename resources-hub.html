Resources Hub

The LIME Network Resources Hub has been developed to enhance information sharing, supporting LIME Network members to deliver initiatives in Indigenous medical and health education, and encourage the development of Indigenous health as a discipline in its own right.

If you would like to add a resource to the Hub, please contact us via lime-network@unimelb.edu.au email
